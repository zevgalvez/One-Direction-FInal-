One Direction: 5 Separate Solo Careers

Harry Styles

“A dream is only a dream... until you decide to make it real”

After leaving One Direction, Harry signed a recording contract with Columbia Records in the first half of 2016. Around this time, he also launched his own record label, Erskine Records. In March of 2017, he announced that his first solo single, "Sign of the Times", would be released in April. After its release, the song went on to peak at number one on the UK Singles Chart and at number four on the Billboard Hot 100. Due to its success, Harry then went on to release the music video in May of 2017. It consisted of Harry flying and walking on water, and won the Brit Award for British Video of the Year [4].

In addition to the music video, Harry released his self-titled debut album was released in May of 2017. The album was influenced by 1970s soft rock, which Harry has deemed one of his favorite genres of music [4]. The album was received relatively well by fans and critics alike.

Harry's second studio album came in December of 2019. Titled "Fine Line" and released by Columbia and Erskine Records, themes in this album include: the initial excitement of meeting someone, breakups, and "having sex and feeling sad", as Harry has said himself. The album has elements of prog-pop, psychedelic pop, folk, soul, funk and indie pop [5]. It has been certified double platinum in the US for combined sales and album-equivalent units of over two million units in the country.
