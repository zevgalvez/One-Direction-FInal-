One Direction: 5 Separate Solo Careers

Home Page

On July 23, 2010, 5 teenage boys were placed in a group together on the X Factor. Not having known it then, those same boys would later form a band named One Direction, and become one of the most popular boybands of all time. After Zayn's departure from the group in 2015 and the group's eventual disbandment in 2016, the 5 talented men each pursued musical careers of their own.

This website will specifically be focusing on each member's solo career, discography, and events they have participated in since departing from One Direction.
